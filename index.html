Discover McDonald's Gaborone

McDonald's Gaborone is your favorite destination for delicious, affordable, and high-quality fast food. Located in the heart of Botswana's capital, we serve thousands of customers every day with a smile. From classic burgers and crispy fries to refreshing drinks and delightful desserts, we bring the iconic McDonald's experience to the people of Gaborone.

This website is your complete guide to our restaurant. Whether you want to learn about our story, explore our services, read what other customers are saying, stay updated with the latest news, or get in touch with us — everything is just a click away.

Why Choose McDonald's Gaborone?

Quality Ingredients

We use 100% beef patties, fresh vegetables, and high-quality chicken. All meals are prepared fresh daily.

Fast & Friendly Service

Our well-trained crew members ensure quick service and a warm welcome every time you visit.

Community Focused

We actively support local schools, youth sports programs, and charity initiatives across Gaborone.

Affordable Prices

Great value meals and combos that suit every budget — from students to large families.

We are open daily from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM
