Our History

1940

McDonald's founded by brothers Richard and Maurice McDonald in San Bernardino, California.McDonalds rich history began in 1940 when brothers Richard and Maurice McDonald opened their first restaurant in San Bernardino, California. Originally a barbecue drive-in, they later revolutionized the food industry in 1948 by creating a simple, efficient self-service system focused on hamburgers, fries, and beverages.

1955

Ray Kroc opens the first franchised McDonald's in Des Plaines, Illinois.In 1955, entrepreneur Ray Kroc opened the first franchised McDonalds restaurant in Des Plaines, Illinois. This marked the beginning of McDonalds extraordinary global expansion. Ray Krocs vision and business model turned McDonalds into a fast-food powerhouse.

Today

Over 40,000 restaurants worldwide serving 70+ million customers daily.Today, McDonalds operates more than 40,000 restaurants worldwide and serves approximately 70 million customers every day.
